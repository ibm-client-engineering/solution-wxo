### Add the skills
Add the skills from the skill catalog, and connect the application:
#### Steps
1. From the home screen, towards the bottom, click the "Add skills from the catalog" box
2. Find and select "JC - Watsonx API" from the list
3. Connect the app
  1. Click the "Connect the app" box on the top right
  2. Enter your bearer token (see [Authentication](/solution-wxo/watsonx/GettingStarted/auth))
  3. Click connect app
4. Add each imported skill from the list

The skills should now be added to your catalog under "JC - Watsonx API" and ready for use directly or in a composite Skill flow.
